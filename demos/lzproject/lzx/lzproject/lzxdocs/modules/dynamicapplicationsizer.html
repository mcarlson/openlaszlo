<DynamicApplicationSizer>

DynamicApplicationSizer: Sizes the application within the given values

Usage: 

         <canvas>
              <DynamicApplicationSizer x="20" width="917" height="726" />   
         </canvas>
         @END

Attributes

maxHeight (default: 726)
 
maxWidth (default: 900)
 
minHeight (default: 620)
 
minWidth (default: 800)
 

Methods

init ()
