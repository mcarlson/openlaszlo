Notes                               for more details  dragstate reference
A state often involves more than one attribute. In this example, the user can drag an item from the shopping cart to the wish list and then back from the wish list to the shopping cart. In dragging mode, the x and y coordinates of the item view are constrained to the x and y coordinates of the mouse. The <dragstate> tag is a specialized state that encapsulates the constraints required for drag and drop.
