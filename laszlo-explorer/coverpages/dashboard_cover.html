Dashboard

This application opens in a window. If you don't see it, you may need to bring that window to the front.

This "universal canvas" brings together, in one seamless application, mail, personal calendar, news feeds, chat, address-book and more. Be sure to play a tune in the media center as you experiment with the other capabilities.

Windows may be moved and resized; however, they also have a "home"—their place of origin. The minimize (-) button in the upper-right corner sends them back, allowing you to easily manage limited screen real-estate.

This is a "concept" demo and is not integrated with back-end data providers. However, by inspecting the code you can see how it could be expanded into a full-featured "portal".
